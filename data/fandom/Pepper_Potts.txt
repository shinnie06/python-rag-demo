Virginia "Pepper" Potts is the CEO of Stark Industries. Originally working as Tony Stark's personal assistant, she would take care of his schedule and perform any task he wished. During this time, Potts became good friends with Stark and soon, they had begun developing romantic feelings for each other. However, neither was able to act on these feelings, as they each feared that the other would not accept them.

When Stark returned from captivity in Afghanistan, Potts was trusted enough by him to see his Iron Man suits and his workshop. She helped Stark defeat Obadiah Stane, who tried to kill Stark, by overloading the Arc Reactor, killing Stane. After Stane's death, Stark made Potts his new business partner, taking Stane's place. Believing his death was imminent, Stark stepped down as CEO and appointed her as his replacement. After Stark defeated Whiplash at the Stark Expo, both Potts and Stark finally admitted their attraction to each other and began a romantic relationship.

During the crisis surrounding the Mandarin's terrorist attacks, Potts was kidnapped by Aldrich Killian and injected with Extremis. She was freed by Stark during the Battle on the Norco and went on to kill Killian herself. Stark cured her of the effects of Extremis, who temporarily gave up being Iron Man just for her.

As the War on Hydra began, the Avengers needed to be remobilized. Without S.H.I.E.L.D. active to run the team, Stark and Potts took over, allowing Captain America to retain field and team leadership while he designed everything else, including creating new armors and rejoining the team himself as an active duty combatant. Sometime after the Ultron Offensive, he and Potts broke off their relationship, which affected Stark and left him wondering why he could not make it work while his father could.

However, shortly after the Avengers Civil War ended, Stark and Potts resumed their relationship, where Stark eventually proposed to her. The two married following the Snap and had a daughter, Morgan Stark. The three of them resided on the Stark Eco-Compound until Stark departed for the Time Heist. Later, Potts joined the Avengers in the Battle of Earth as Rescue, wearing the Mark XLIX armor, when Thanos' forces invaded. After Stark died by her side, Potts was present at his funeral, where she set adrift Stark's first Arc Reactor onto a wreath into their residential lakeside.

## Biography

 

### Early Career

#### Meeting Aldrich Killian

Virginia Potts earned a BA in accounting. Afterwards, she became a model, but desired more challenge from life than empty days of posing and smiling. As a result, Potts eventually left her modeling career to pursue a corporate profession.

Potts' early jobs involved her working alongside Aldrich Killian, though she did not enjoy the experience as Killian would continually flirt with her. 

### Stark Industries

#### Working with Tony Stark

 
Potts was later hired by Stark Industries and began working as an administrative assistant in their finance department. One day, she discovered a numbers error on some projections and reported it to her supervisor but he dismissed her concerns, as did the general manager, Folan, who also fired Potts when she persisted. Undeterred, Potts made her way to the CEO's office and personally confronted Tony Stark with her findings. When security tried to remove her, she lied that she had pepper spray in her purse, prompting Stark to give her the nickname "Pepper." Impressed with her actions, Stark offered Potts a job on the executive floor as his personal assistant, which she accepted. 

In her new position, Potts kept Stark on track in spite of his chaotic lifestyle by efficiently managing her boss's business and personal affairs. Unfortunately, this included requiring her to send Stark's one-night-stands home the next morning. Overtime, Potts and Stark became trusted friends, though Stark would frequently forget when her birthday was, prompting Potts to get herself a birthday gift with his money.

## Powers and Abilities

### Powers

 
*Rescue Armor Capabilities: In the five years after the Snap, Tony Stark created an Iron Man Armor for his wife. He planned to reveal it to her as a present, but found their daughter, Morgan Stark, playing with the helmet. He carefully hid it back in the house and went of his mission. When Thanos attacked Earth, Potts found the armor and went to Avengers Compound to fight in the battle. She fought side by side with her husband and the other heroes.
**Superhuman Strength: Although she mostly used her distance as a strategy during the short time she wore the Rescue Armor, she was granted increased strength which allowed her to defeat many enemies during the Battle of Earth.
**Superhuman Durability: Wearing the Rescue Armor, Pepper was granted an incredible durability to kinetic impacts as well as exposure to energy blasts or any other form of enemy fire. This durability is placed on display during the Battle for Earth when she along with the other heroes battling against the forces of Thanos were abruptly bombarded by a devastating missile strike ordered by the Mad Titan.
**Superhuman Speed: While flying, Potts was able to fly at speeds equal to War Machine and almost as fast as Iron Man. She also flew from her house to Avengers Compound in a very short time.
**Superhuman Agility: While using the armor, Potts is able to do various ariel maneuvers as in catching Spider-Man in mid air and going back to back with Iron Man to defeat enemies. She could even land in a pose on the ground.
**Superhuman Stamina: With the armor on, Potts battled through the whole Battle of Earth without taking a break.
**Superhuman Reflexes: The armor's HUD system allows Potts to dodge incoming attacks such as the attacks that Thanos ordered his troops to fire. She also sensed a Necrocraft behind her and quickly turned around to destroy it.
**Flight: With the repulsor technology included in the Rescue Armor, Pepper was able to utilize the repulsors in her suit to enable flight. She flew all the way from her house in Upstate New York to the Battle at Avengers Compound.
**Power Suit: ]] Like Stark's armors, the suit contains a heads up display within the helmet to allow Potts to analyze the environment. She can utilize the display for targeting assistance. Besides flight, the repulsor technology in her suit enables Potts to battle offensively by unleashing energy blasts from her hands, similar to Iron Man and War Machine. The armor also had a unibeam built in. The beam allowed her to duo with her husband and shoot a Necrocraft.

### Former Powers

 
 
*Artificially Enhanced Physiology: Thanks to Extremis, Potts temporarily displayed various superhuman abilities, such as strength, agility, and enhanced healing factor, and the ability to generate intense heat. The Extremis may even cause the user to explode if it doesn't suit the host right. However, Tony Stark was able to negate all of its effects and had the Extremis removed from her body.
**Superhuman Strength: ]] Extremis enhanced Potts' muscles to the point of being able to make a hole in one of the Iron Man Armors and rip its arm to use it as a weapon.
**Superhuman Agility: Extremis enhanced Potts' agility to the point where she was able to effortlessly dodge a repulsor attack. She even used Tony Stark as leverage to leap off of him and punch an Iron Man Armor mid air and then slam it on the ground while landing perfectly.
**Superhuman Stamina: The physiological enhancements brought by Extremis allowed Potts to join Iron Man's battle against Aldrich Killian despite having just survived a fall and explosion that would have killed her without the serum.
**Superhuman Reflexes: Potts was able to easily dodge a repulsor blast from one of the armors in the Iron Legion controlled by J.A.R.V.I.S.
**Regenerative Healing Factor: Extremis allowed Potts to heal at a remarkable rate. She managed to survive a great fall, and walk away with no injuries at all. The healing factor is so remarkable that it is able to regenerate severed limbs and also take away mental illnesses.
**Heat Generation: Hosts of Extremis are able to consciously raise the temperature of parts of their bodies. The effect can be controlled to the extent that it might only scorch skin, or increased to the point where it can cause steel to turn into a molten state. Potts used this heat to easily punch through an Iron Man Armor.

### Abilities

 
 
*Master Businessperson: ]] Pepper Potts is an exceptionally capable businesswoman with a very forceful personality. Ultimately, Tony Stark entrusted her with his family's company and she expanded it from Los Angeles to New York to Washington, D.C. in the United States alone. Potts kept the company from bankruptcy after Stark had taken away the weapons program, which was Stark Industries biggest department.
 
*Combatant: Potts was able to hold her own against opponents whenever she was in battle. After getting injected with Extremis, she was able to perform many fighting techniques that were theoretically enhanced by the serum, but nevertheless she was able to kill Aldrich Killian. During the Battle of Earth, Potts used her Rescue Armor to perform many effective aerial attacks, even teaming up with her husband, Iron Man, at one point.

## Equipment

### Iron Man Armors

*Mark XLII Armor: While Tony Stark's Mansion was being attacked. Stark sent his armor to pick up Potts. Potts then used the armor to save Maya Hansen. Once she was safe, Stark sent the armor back to him.
*Mark IX Armor: Pepper Potts ended the Battle on the Norco using the right arm of this armor, previously destroyed by her, to kill Aldrich Killian.
*Mark XLIX Armor: During the Battle of Earth, Pepper joined the fight alongside many of the Avengers and their allies in a suit much like Tony's and displayed considerable proficiency in operating it, despite this being the first official instance she is shown using it.

### Other Equipment

*Extremis: Potts was injected with Extremis by Aldrich Killian. This gave her unnatural abilities which she eventually used to defeat Killian. She then got it removed by Tony Stark after.

### Vehicles

*Stark Industries Private Jet: On the way back from the Duel of Monaco, Potts and Tony Stark took the jet. He made her a meal while Potts watched the news about Whiplash.

## Facilities

*Tony Stark's Mansion: As the personal assistant to Tony Stark, Potts spent a great deal of time at his mansion. Along with her various executive tasks, Potts kept the house in order, doing anything from dry cleaning to making sure Stark's one night stands left at the appropriate time.
*Stark Industries Headquarters: Potts first found the Iron Monger plans on Stane's computer in the headquarters. Once she became CEO, she got her own office where she did her work. Tony Stark regularly visited her, and Aldrich Killian had a pitch meeting with her.
*Walt Disney Concert Hall: Potts attended the Stark Industries charity event, likely because of her close connection to Tony who was not formally invited. She wore the dress she bought herself with Stark's money as a birthday gift and later danced, and nearly kissed, with the man himself.
*United States Capitol: Potts sat in the gallery as she attended Stark's Senate committee hearing regarding the military's attempts to get the Iron Man suit technology.
*Stark Tower: Potts and Tony Stark worked together to make the tower all clean energy. Stark remarked how she can have 12% of the credit. They were then approached by Phil Coulson, who gave Stark information on the Avengers Initiative to prepare for the Chitauri Invasion. After the Battle of New York, Potts helped Stark remodel for the Avengers.
*Avengers Compound: Potts prepared to announce Spider-Man as the new member of the Avengers when Parker denied the offer. Tony Stark then proposed to Potts to fill the conference time.
*Stark Eco-Compound: After Tony Stark retired, the two moved to upstate New York where they lived in an Eco-Compound and had a child, Morgan.

### Prisons

*Aldrich Killian's Mansion: Potts was kidnapped by Aldrich Killian, both as leverage for Stark and as a personal trophy for Killian. During her imprisonment, Potts was injected with Extremis and went through two phases before she was moved to the Norco.
*Norco: Still undergoing the Extremis process, Potts was transferred to the ship to watch the execution of President Matthew Ellis. She escaped when Stark and Rhodes launched a rescue mission, after surviving several explosions and a 200-foot fall.
